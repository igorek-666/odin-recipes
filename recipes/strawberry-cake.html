Strawberry Cake

Description

This strawberry cake completely blew me away. After years of mediocre from-scratch strawberry cakes, my expectations were pretty low. It was time to taste test my efforts. Biting into the first pastel-pink forkful was the moment of truthâ€¦

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Jell-O
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Milk
  • Strawberries
  • Vanilla

Steps

  1. Beat the butter, sugar, and Jell-O together. Add the eggs.
  2. Combine the dry ingredients.
  3. Gradually the dry mixture into the wet mixture, alternating with the milk.
  4. Add the remaining ingredients and transfer the batter to prepared cake pans.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick comes out clean.
